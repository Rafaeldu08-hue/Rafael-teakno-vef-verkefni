The Fastest Racing Series

Formula 1 is the world's premier motorsport competition, featuring the fastest single-seater racing cars and the most skilled drivers on the planet. With races held on iconic circuits across six continents, F1 combines cutting-edge technology, strategy, and extraordinary athleticism.

Technology & Innovation

F1 vehicles are engineering marvels, featuring hybrid power units that produce over 1000 horsepower, advanced aerodynamics, and sophisticated onboard computer systems. Every component is designed to maximize performance while adhering to strict regulations that ensure competitive balance among teams.

Championship Glory

Drivers from around the world compete in a 24-race championship season to earn the prestigious World Driver's Championship title. Every point matters, with dramatic battles at each circuit testing the limits of both machine and driver in pursuit of ultimate victory.

Mercedes-AMG Petronas
  • Mercedes is one of the most successful teams in F1 history, with multiple world championships and record-breaking performances. Known for innovation and excellence, they have dominated the 1.6L turbo hybrid era.
  • The team features talented drivers and an engineering crew that consistently pushes the boundaries of performance and technology in Formula 1 racing.
Scuderia Ferrari
  • Ferrari is the legendary Italian team with the longest history in Formula 1, known for passion, innovation, and distinctive red cars. They have won 16 Constructors' Championships throughout their storied career.
  • The Scuderia has produced some of F1's greatest drivers and vehicles, maintaining heritage while competing at the highest level of motorsport.
Red Bull Racing
  • Red Bull Racing has emerged as a powerhouse in modern Formula 1, dominating the recent seasons with innovative car design and exceptional driving talent. The team has won multiple championships and demonstrates consistent excellence.
  • Known for pushing technical boundaries and developing world-class talent, Red Bull continues to set the standard for competitiveness in contemporary Formula 1 racing.
McLaren
  • McLaren is a British racing team with a rich heritage dating back to 1963. Known for innovative design and remarkable achievements, McLaren has won numerous championships and produced legendary drivers.
  • The team combines cutting-edge technology with strategic prowess, consistently challenging for victories and podium finishes in the competitive Formula 1 championship.
